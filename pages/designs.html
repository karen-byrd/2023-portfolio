Born To Ride

For the past 2 years I have been helping a non-profit, Born To Ride. I have created flyers about six times a year, certificates for competitions, and business cards. I developed their first website with wix.com and am in the process of revamping the site. I created the border with individual skateboards because the non-profit is a skateboarding ministry.

Paws & Claws

Paws and Claws Holistic Animal Clinic was given as a project for my Adobe Illustrator class.Throughout the term we developed the logo, dos and don'ts of how to use the logo, stationary, and business card. Through the term I learned how to create and manipulate logos. Our final project was to make a brand book to show everything the customer would need moving forward in the business.
