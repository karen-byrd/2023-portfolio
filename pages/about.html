My dream

I have desired to create experiences for people to have outstanding websites. I want everyone who visits them to know how things work, what expectations will come when browsing, and a sense of familiarity. There is nothing over the top about my designs (unless you desire unique).


I learned UX Design with Kenzie Academy. Which created the passion to help businesses and clients create wonderful and memoriable websites. My experience is limited to class projects and daily challenges. I aquired a BS in Game Programming and Development from SNHU, but my desire is to take my coding knowledge and utilized it for creative websites. I love the idea of creating games so people can esacpe reality for a while. One of my greatest joys to sit in my game chair and loose for myself for hours, discovering unknow worlds and characters.


My true calling is helping people get the most out of technology in todays worlds. I want everyone to be knowledgable with where things are going and how to circumvent what is to come. I will contine to make modern sites that people won't have to scan and re-train themselves on how to flow around the site. Truth... I don't have any real world experience except for my dad's non-profit. I am trying to change this by getting my master in Web Design. I want to make people happy with taking their business to new places.
